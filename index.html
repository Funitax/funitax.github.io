English BootCamp

Project Brief:

During these two weeks of English bootcamp, we realized two projects that allowed us to deepen our level of English and to put ourselves in the shoes of managers, designers and developers to learn how to exchange in a professional setting in English. During our first project with Rémi, Ryan, Chabanne and myself. We created a company called BabaHome. The goal of this business was to sell kitchen products and offer our services to other businesses. This project went very well, we learned how to work as a team and especially how to give constructive and professional speeches to convince our clients to collaborate with us. During the second week, accompanied by Zainab and Yasmine, we innovated a food truck that offered only organic products with table service, take-away meals and even home deliveries. At the end of this week we presented this project to a jury and we put ourselves in a real situation in order to gain confidence in ourselves and to express ourselves in English to offer our services to customers.
